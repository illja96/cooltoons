Lois is a hard-working mom who often gets stuck with the crummy graveyard shift at Sheltered Shrubs Hospital, where she’s a Nurse. With a no-nonsense approach and a healthy dose of humor, Lois does a great job raising her kids solo. Not one to put on airs, Lois sometimes forgets to candy-coat things that are particularly harsh. For that reason, she could be accused of mothering with a "tough love" attitude. But her kids always know they come first, since their mom’s is always the loudest voice cheering in the audience and the first one grumbling voluntarily for carpool. With Lois Foutley, flattery will get you nowhere. She prefers brutal honesty with a Diet Soda chaser.
