Lightyears ahead of her peers in the cosmetic application skill-set, Courtney Gripling takes her role as Most Popular Girl very seriously. Can Courtney Gripling pluck a total "nobody" from the dregs of everyday life and make her a Popular Girl? She intends to find out. But practical joke aside, Courtney's intrigue with Ginger is real. Courtney is envies the rock-solid friendship that Ginger seems to share with her friends and is baffled by her ethics.
